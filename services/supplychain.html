trade smart supply chain

Trade Smart Supplies is a leading supplier of various products in Zambia, including goats, maize meal, bread, and chemical products for mining operations. Effective supply chain management is essential to ensure that the products are delivered on time, in good condition, and at the right price to meet customer demand. Here's an overview of the supply chain for each of these products.

Goat Product Supply

Trade Smart Supplies sources goats from local farmers in the Southern Province of Zambia. The company ensures that the goats are of good quality and free from any health issues. The goats are transported to a central collection point where they are sorted, weighed, and examined before being transported to the border town of Kasumbalesa. Customs and immigration officials clear the goats, and they are then loaded onto trucks and transported to various markets in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), including the capital city of Kinshasa. Effective communication and planning are crucial to ensure that the goats are transported safely and efficiently and that the right quantity of goats is available to meet customer demand.

Maize Meal (mealie meal) Supply

Trade Smart Supplies sources maize grain from local farmers in Zambia. The grain is transported to a central milling plant where it is processed into maize meal, which is a staple food in the region. The maize meal is then packaged and transported to Kasumbalesa, where it is sold to wholesalers and retailers. Effective inventory management is critical to ensure that the right quantity of maize meal is delivered on time to meet customer demand. The company also ensures that the maize meal is of good quality and packaged in a way that preserves its freshness and nutritional value.

Baked Product Supply

Trade Smart Supplies sources ingredients, including flour, yeast, sugar, and other additives, from local suppliers and neighboring countries. The ingredients are transported to a central bakery where they are mixed and baked into bread. The bread is then packaged and transported to various retail outlets, including supermarkets and convenience stores, within Chililabombwe. Effective inventory management is critical to ensure that the right quantity of bread is delivered on time to meet customer demand. The company also ensures that the bread is of good quality and packaged in a way that preserves its freshness and flavor.

Chemical Supply to mines

Trade Smart Supplies sources raw materials, including sulfuric acid, Sulphur, and other chemicals, from local suppliers or importers. The company has a central distribution center where the raw materials are mixed and packaged into the required chemical products. These products are then transported to mining operations throughout Zambia, which often require specialized transport equipment, including tanker trucks and helicopters. Effective communication and planning are crucial to ensure that the right chemical products are delivered to the right locations at the right time to support the mining operations. The company also ensures that the chemical products are of good quality and meet the specific requirements of each mining operation.
