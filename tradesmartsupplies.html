About Company

Trade Smart Supplies

Trade Smart Supplies Limited is a Consultancy and Supplier company based in Chililabombwe, Zambia and have partners based in Lubumbashi and Kolwezi, DRC. We are located just an hour away from the major border town of Kasumbalesa between Zambia and the DRC. One of the busiest and most important border regions in the SADC area. And also just a few hours away from the major mining towns in the Copperbelt such as Chingola, Kitwe, Ndola, Mufulira and Solwezi.

Experts In

Consultancy

Trade Smart Supplies provides export and import consultation services to help businesses expand their international trade operations. Our experienced team offers guidance and support throughout the entire import/export process, from finding the right suppliers or buyers to navigating customs regulations and documentation.

Supply Chain

We are specialised in the supply of mealie meal (maize meal) to Kasumbalesa and facilitate the commerce of Goats from the Southern Provice of Zambia to Kasumbalesa and the mines on the Copperbelt. We are also specialised in chemical supply and other products to mines.

Bakery

We are producers of baked products thats is produced from wheat flour that is grown in Belgium which is grown using organic fertilzer which gives it a special taste. We supply our baked products to the mines and within Chililabombwe town.
